## **1. Understanding Coffee Plant Requirements**  

### **Climate & Temperature**  
- Thrives in **warm, humid climates**.  
- Ideal temperature: **18-24Â°C (65-75Â°F)**.  
- Prefers **partial shade to filtered sunlight**.  
- Cannot tolerate **frost or extreme heat**.  

### **Soil Type & Preparation**  
- Requires **rich, well-draining soil**.  
- Soil pH: **6.0 - 6.5** (slightly acidic).  
- Add **organic compost and mulch** for better nutrition.  

### **Water Requirements**  
- Needs **consistent moisture**, but avoid overwatering.  
- Water **2-3 times per week**; reduce in winter.  
- Ensure **proper drainage** to prevent root rot.

### **Step 2: Seed Selection & Germination**  
- **Use fresh, ripe coffee seeds (cherries)** from a reliable source.  
- Soak seeds in **lukewarm water for 24 hours** to enhance germination.  
- Plant in **moist soil**, covering lightly.  
- Keep at **warm temperatures (20-25Â°C)**; germination takes **2-3 months**.  

---

### **Step 3: Planting Coffee Seeds or Seedlings**  

#### **Direct Sowing (Outdoor Planting)**  
1. **Best time to plant**: Early **spring**.  
2. **Spacing**: Maintain **4-5 feet between plants**.  
3. **Soil Preparation**: Mix **compost, sand, and peat moss** for better aeration.  
4. **Mulching**: Helps retain soil moisture and control weeds.  

#### **Growing in Containers (Indoor or Balcony Gardening)**  
- Use a **large pot (12-16 inches deep)** with drainage holes.  
- Keep **near a window** with **indirect sunlight** (6+ hours).  
- Use **well-draining, acidic soil mix** with compost.  

---

### **Step 4: Watering & Fertilization**  
- Water **regularly**, but let the topsoil dry slightly between watering.  
- Use **organic compost or coffee-based fertilizers** every **6-8 weeks**.  
- **Magnesium and potassium-rich fertilizers** improve bean production.
